Red Velvet Cake

What is Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cake is a layered red-colored cake with a white icing and a subtle chocolate flavor. The cake contains such a small amount of cocoa, somewhere in the range of 2 to 3 tablespoons per 2½ cups flour, that it really does not taste like a chocolate cake.

ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
  • red food coloring, as desired
  • ½ cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Prepare cake according to package directions, substituting half of the water called for with buttermilk (approximately 1/2 cup). Stir in pudding mix and food coloring.

  3. Step 3

    4. Pour into cake pan(s) and bake according to package directions.
